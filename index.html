Episode 0.9.3: hacker culture touching the normal world

July 29, 2011

This week we talked a lot about how hacker culture, with our language, thought processes and motivations, interacts with “regular people” (if such a thing exists). We covered:

  • How we all got into programming
  • Motivating young developers
  • How to teach someone to program
  • Working with design agencies
  • The recent BT block force on Newzbin2
  • Hacker lingo, culture, geek-speak and interacting with people who don’t share our interests
  • Business thinking
